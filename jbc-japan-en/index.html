Company Concept

Japan Business Consulting Co., Ltd. is a comprehensive Japan–China consulting firm headquartered in Ebisu, Osaka — one of the city’s most dynamic political and commercial centers.

With a capital of 99 million yen, we integrate expertise and networks from both Japan and China to deliver high-quality, professional consulting services that meet the diverse needs of our clients.

Our services encompass a wide range of fields, including:

  • Corporate and industrial visit coordination
  • Business exchange events and exhibition planning
  • Medical and beauty service coordination
  • Real estate introductions and investment support
  • Marketing and business development
  • Company establishment and registration assistance
  • Patent and intellectual property consulting

Through our sincere, client-centered approach, we aim to promote stronger economic and cultural collaboration between Japan and China, and contribute to the ongoing growth and prosperity of both nations.

In recent years, Japan’s inbound tourism market has undergone significant change.

To support international visitors in creating memorable experiences during their stay, we offer a variety of travel-related services, including professional tour guide dispatch and customized local programs.

In partnership with major travel agencies in China, we design and operate experience-based tours that cater to individual travel purposes — such as VIP private trips, golf and ski tours, medical checkups, beauty and wellness experiences, and factory visits.

Our experienced Japanese travel advisors and Chinese-speaking staff provide attentive support for guests throughout Japan.

Additionally, we own and manage hotels and traditional “Kyomachiya” guesthouses in the Kansai region, offering a unique blend of Japanese culture and comfort.

Our expertise also extends to hotel management, property design and construction, supervision, and consulting, allowing us to provide comprehensive and integrated hospitality services.

Through these initiatives, Japan Business Consulting Co., Ltd. continues to build meaningful connections across industries and borders — helping clients and partners achieve success while contributing to the continued development of Japan’s global business and tourism landscape.

Message from the President

Our company was originally established in October 2011 in Osaka under the name Japan Business Tourist Co., Ltd., primarily focusing on VIP private travel, including golf, ski trips, medical checkups, beauty treatments, and factory tours. To expand our business operations, we restructured in 2016 as Japan Business Consulting Co., Ltd., with a capital of 99 million yen. Our core services now include hotel and guesthouse management in Japan, building design and construction supervision, and comprehensive consulting services. We also provide support in real estate investment, management, sales, leasing, and brokerage. Currently, we own and operate hotels and traditional Kyomachiya guesthouses in the Kansai region, and we are in the process of developing our own hotel property. Through these initiatives, we aim to provide high-quality services and contribute to the growth of Japan’s tourism, hospitality, and business sectors.

Hotel and Guesthouse Management

We operate and manage hotels as well as renovated traditional Kyomachiya guesthouses in Kyoto. These accommodations offer full-house rentals, providing guests with an authentic and private experience of Japanese culture, alongside our professionally managed hotel properties.

Real Estate Services

Our real estate business encompasses:

  • Investment, management, sales, leasing, and brokerage of properties across Japan
  • Planning, design, construction supervision, and consulting for buildings
  • Introduction and advisory services for investment properties, including single-room apartments, entire buildings, apartment complexes, and commercial buildings
  • Professional consultation on property valuation, sale, investment, and commercial real estate

Through these services, we provide comprehensive support to clients seeking strategic real estate investment and property management opportunities in Japan.
