Inbound Services

Personal Travel

We coordinate inspection tours and travel for VIP clients, ensuring a seamless and stress-free experience abroad. Our comprehensive services cover not only hotel and flight arrangements but also local transportation, dining venues, and other on-site arrangements to provide a fully tailored travel experience.

Health Checkups

From routine medical exams to PET scans, we partner with a variety of medical institutions and offer outsourced services, providing a wide range of service models from client acquisition to appointment coordination.

Anti-Aging & Cosmetic Services

We support clients in selecting clinics specializing in dermatology, plastic surgery, and cosmetic procedures. Our services cover treatments such as vitamin injections, eyelid surgery, breast augmentation, wrinkle and sagging care, liposuction, and umbilical stem cell therapies. We also provide information on exclusive promotions and special offers.

Hotel & Guesthouse Operations

Image is for illustrative purposes

Hotel & Guesthouse Management

We operate a renovated traditional machiya guesthouse in Kyoto, offering the entire townhouse as a private accommodation. Each room is equipped with air conditioning, a television, an electric kettle, and a private bathroom with a bathtub, complimentary toiletries, and a hairdryer. Located in a popular area of Kyoto, the guesthouse is highly rated by travelers for its authentic experience and comfort.

Building Design & Construction Management

We renovate traditional machiya townhouses in Kyoto into guesthouse accommodations. Each room is fully equipped with air conditioning, a television, an electric kettle, and a private bathroom with a bathtub, complimentary toiletries, and a hairdryer.

Real Estate Investment, Sales & Brokerage

We provide a wide range of investment properties, including studio apartments, entire apartment buildings, multi-unit complexes, and commercial buildings. Our services cover property valuation, sales inquiries, tenant recruitment, rental management, and full support for property sales.

Commercial Property Leasing & Sales

As a trusted partner in the real estate business, we offer unparalleled access to property information and a proven track record while ensuring strict confidentiality. With over 2,000 property listings and diverse transaction experience, we provide professional consultation for commercial and investment properties, including buildings, apartments, and business real estate acquisitions.
